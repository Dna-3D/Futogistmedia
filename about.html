About FUTO GIST

Your trusted campus business and entertainment hub

Our Story

FUTO GIST was born from a simple idea: to connect the vibrant FUTO community through a single, comprehensive platform. As a student-run business, we understand the unique needs of campus life and the challenges students face in discovering services, events, and opportunities.

What started as a small initiative to help fellow students find reliable services has grown into the go-to platform for everything happening on and around FUTO campus. We bridge the gap between students and local businesses, event organizers and attendees, and the campus community as a whole.

Our mission is simple: to make campus life easier, more connected, and more enjoyable for everyone in the FUTO community.
